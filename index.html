The Context

Why D-HEIRS?

Addressing the challenges of manual reporting at LICH-AMBA PHCU.

LICH-AMBA PHCU

Established in 2003 E.C., serving as the primary health hub for Lich-Amba, Arada, and Lereba. We coordinate OPD, Lab, Pharmacy, and the critical Health Extension Program.

The Problem

  • Delayed Reporting
  • Data Loss & Damage
  • Inaccurate Records

The Solution

  • Digital Data Entry
  • Cloud Storage
  • Real-time Analytics

Our Mission

To ensure every citizen has access to primary health care through a network of dedicated Health Extension Workers (HEWs), empowered by digital tools.

System Workflow

From Field to National Report

01

Data Collection

HEW

Health Extension Workers visit households in Lich-Amba, Arada, and Lereba. They use the mobile-friendly web app to register families and record health data (Immunization, MCH, etc.).

02

Review & Validation

Coordinator Linkage Focal

HEW Coordinators review the raw data. Linkage Focal Persons validate consistency and convert community data into statistical formats.

03

Reporting & Analysis

HMIS Officer Supervisor

HMIS Officers generate final reports for DHIS2. Supervisors and Admins use dashboards to monitor real-time performance and trends.
